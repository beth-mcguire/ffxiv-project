My Favorite People

Flynn Fox

My best friend since three years of age. He has done more for me than anyone in this damned world. We were outcasts and misfits. No one wanted 'those dirty Miqo'te' and we were treated no different from gutter rats. We were kicked, spat on, detested, and ridiculed by the local Hyur. So, we turned to thieving no longer caring about morality. We'd use money to fly to the Golden Saucer gambling to our heart's desires sneaking in sips of alcohol. Thancred protected us once as kids, and I don't think he remembers it, but Flynn and I are eternally grateful.

Flynn became a top ranking officer of the Twin Adder and playfully hits on me to get a reaction. I can't imagine my life without him even if he can be wildly infuriating. We made a promise as kids: we will always be a linkpearl apart. Putting all our goals and duties aside, the other's needs outweigh our own as living a life without the other is meaningless.

I will always be here for you, Flynn. Nothing in this wretched world will ever change that. Not even you chiding me for my bleak baking skills. You are my best friend, my family, my inspiration and the light that keeps me on the right path. Thank you. For everything.

Thancred Waters

What could possibly be said about the legendary Thancred Waters? He's an idiot. A flirt. A manwhore. Yet, despite his flaws, I value him as much as I do Flynn. Drinking with him leads to unforgettable adventures that I wish I could forget. I'd do anything for this love sick puppy. Similar to my past, he also wandered the streets in Limsa Lominsa as a purse snatcher, making a lucky break and was under the care of a wealthy man. A luxury I'm glad I didn't have.

It's difficult to define our relationship. Thancred is Thancred. He is perfect the way he is even if he loves to send me flying into walls spewing out thick strands of blood. Yet in those times, he would cradle me afterwards and wash away the blood. He trained me when I asked for help improving my prowess with a blde and not once did he let me down. We often make wagers and the loser has to buy the next rounds of drinks. Guess who wins the most wagers? Me! Guess who is growing broke because he's an idiot? Thancred! You'll get there, buddy.

I love you, Thancred. Truly. With my entire being. May we live our years getting drunk in random taverns forgetting what in the seven hells we were talking about but vividly recalling our special nights. Sometimes I convince myself I could fall in love with you. And then I drink.

Estinien Varlineau

A dumbass I like more than I should. Estinien enchanted my heart after a couple days forced into his company. Starting as a condescending asshole, he quickly grew on me once he showed a playful side, even if he is obsessed with murdering anything that wrongs him.

Possessed by Nidhogg after our heroic endeavors, I was worried I'd never get another chance to see him, but luck proved me wrong. However, once everything was over, he left without a word. Twice. And then pretended nothing ever happened and we were good friends again. Estinien gets his own category. I hate him and I love him. I need therapy.

You saved my life, you bastard. From fleeting loneliness. From the empire. I love you in ways I cannot comprehend. I know you live for the thrill of the hunt, adventures and with a lone wolf nature, we wouldn't work long term. Just, don't make the next time I see you be another year and a half. I will always care about you. Oh, and a word of advice: when you go to open your mouth, just don't.

Alphinaud Leveilleur

This kid has literally been with me from the start of my dastardly adventures and I'd take a bullet for him. Since I first met him, he has grown so much as a person becoming one of the youngest, brightest strategists I've gotten the pleasure of knowing. He's gotten well acquainted with Aymeric, and the Lord Commander has been so kind to allow him command of some units while closely monitoring him.

I wouldn't be here if not for you, my friend. Your caring nature and desire to come to a resolution has saved all our asses on numerous occasions. When you laugh, I laugh. When you cry, I cry. When you are as energized as a squirrel, so am I. Your personality is addicting and you're gonna go far, Alphy. I will always be there for you any time you request me. You have always said I was your hero, but Alphy, you are MY hero and I love that about you.

You, Thancred, and Alisaie have made my time as a Scion unforgettable. Every game we played, thing we said and adventure we embarked on will forever be etched into my memory. We all have shared trauma, but also shared happiness. Our emotions are linked by threads blind to the human eye. Thank you for being you. Thank you for having the courage to find the words when the rest of us stood dumbfounded. Thank you for continuing your knowledge to ensure we'd be safe. Thank you, truly, for being the greatest person in the world. We would be naught if not for you. Thank you. Thank you. From the bottom of my heart, thank you.

Alisaie Leveilleur

I am obsessed with her sassy humor and enjoy when we tagteam picking on Alphinaud, provoking major reactions from him. Of every Scion, Flynn excluded, Alisaie's care for me shone through her. She defended me, cried for me, fought others if she felt they were lying or thinking little of me. She is my protector in a different way. She makes me feel loved, important, and always brings me out of a funk simply by talking. She felt Thancred sucked training me and proved to be a better mentor. She had me train four hours a day until I could disarm her. I gained muscle too, ha!

Alisaie is my confidant through and through. She knew about Estinien and Thancred, offering bits of advice and not once did she judge me. At least not visibly. Never wanting to be apart, she threw out the idea all the Scions go in for a large house, and we did at that! We even set up an Aetheryte and keep our linkpearls embedded at all times. If not for her, the Scions wouldn't be as close as they are. She was usually the voice of reason and held the most passion. I'm indebted to her.

Alisaie, you are my best lady friend and that will literally never change. Your personality is intoxicating. Your love for me has shown through every time we're together. There were times on various brinks of death, I recalled moments of you, making a sliver of hope shine through the darkness. You are on par with Thancred and Flynn. May we never part again and if we do, may we return back home, happy and in one piece. I love you, my sister from another mister.

About Z'valiyah Iskani

Born into poverty with no parents, I had to learn everything either from observations or on my own. Having a valid bias towards the Hyur, I kept my distance and stayed close to Flynn. Thancred beat up some Hyurs throwing rocks at us when we were little on multiple occasions. We called him our hero in shadows. I had to have been 10 making him 14. I caught his name, but that was it. Later when I formally met him, I thanked him but he claimed to have saved many Miqo'te, not specifically recalling me. It wasn't until a few years later he said he lied and I was the only Miqo'te he went out of his way to protect. Claiming my beauty captivated him. Idiot. Creepy, too. Childhood was rough, having to pickpocket thieves or take on degrading jobs to afford an inn room.

Wanting to make up for wronging others, I embraced the Warrior of Light title, saving people rather than harming them. I got well acquainted with the Scions and volunteered to help them with their endeavors. Nearly losing my life countless times was exhilarating. Being a white mage, it was up to me to keep everyone alive and nearly failed a couple times, but thankfully the gods favored my team. I will do what I can to keep them and the world from falling into chaos and destruction. Our home is a place of love and beauty and I will stop at nothing to put an end to those threatening to take it.

I suffer from a couple insecurities. I used to get ripped on for my atrocious food poisoning levels of baking and worked effortlessly to perfect my skill when I fell hopelessly in love with Thancred. It still feels like a horrible nightmare because it's Thancred, but I can't help it. He compliments me in every way imaginable. Flynn looks forward to his birthdays now, knowing he'll get something edible as do the other Scions. Can the world just fuck off with all their problems and allow me one year of normalcy? It's never that easy. This time, I'll be prepared for anything and hopefully continue to save the lives of those I hold dear to me. Flynn, Alphinaud, Alisaie, Thancred, Estinien, Aymeric, Tataru, Krille, Y'shtola and Urianger. May we all do our best and put a permanent end to war and suffering. Together, we can accomplish anything. May we continue to fight with the strength of every Scion. For those we have lost. For those we can yet save.
